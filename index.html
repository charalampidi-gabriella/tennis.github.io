About Me

Hi, I'm Gavriella Charalampidi and my tennis journey began at 4 years old. By 8, I was competing in tournaments, and at 11, I became the number one player in Greece. Representing my country, I reached the finals of summer camps, and at 15, I started playing in ITF tournaments, reaching a world ranking of 245 and a UTR of 10.26. My dedication earned me full-ride scholarships to Division 1 schools, where I further honed my abilities on and off the court.

At the University of Nevada, I embraced leadership roles, which enriched my understanding of sports administration and strengthened my passion for mentoring and teaching.

My extensive experience as a player and leader fuels my passion for coaching and sports management. I love sharing my knowledge and helping others develop their skills. Teaching the values of dedication, perseverance, and teamwork is at the core of my coaching philosophy. Whether on the court or in the community, I am committed to making a positive impact and inspiring the next generation of athletes.
